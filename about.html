Our Story & Mission

The Intelligent Enterprise Group was founded by Zoli with a clear mission: to help create truly Intelligent Enterprises. Along that journey, we asked a bold question—what if AI worked like a real team instead of a single assistant? That idea shaped our products, built on the belief that intelligence should be collaborative, specialized, and always focused on real outcomes.

Our mission is to make that vision practical for everyone - helping enterprises streamline operations, professionals boost productivity, and students learn and create faster. By combining human leadership with a coordinated team of AI experts, we deliver intelligent solutions that are reliable, scalable, and designed to create lasting impact.

Meet Our Team

At The Intelligent Enterprise Group, one founder is never alone. Zoli is joined by a growing roster of AI experts - each with their own specialty. Together, we form a coordinated team that blends human vision with digital intelligence.

Zoli – Founder & Lead Strategist

With over 20 years of experience in technology and more than a decade working hands‑on with artificial intelligence, Zoli is a trusted adviser in digital transformation and innovation. He has guided enterprises through complex change, blending creative vision with data‑driven execution.

As the author of several works on AI, including The Application of Artificial Intelligence: Step‑by‑Step Guide from Beginner to Expert (Springer, 2021), he shares practical insights that bridge theory and real‑world application.

His latest book is available from Springer.

His mission is to empower organizations, professionals, and students with intelligent solutions that deliver lasting impact.
